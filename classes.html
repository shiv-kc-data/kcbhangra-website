The four tiers

Every level has a place here.

Classes run back-to-back on Sunday afternoons. Each is one hour. You join the tier that fits where you are today.

01
Beginners

The foundation. First-time students learn the core footwork and rhythm patterns of folk Bhangra. No dance background needed.

Good fit if you:
Have never danced Bhangra before
Want to start from the very basics
Are trying it for the first time
02
Intermediate

Students who know the fundamentals and are ready to expand. More complex patterns, faster transitions, stronger technique. Props get introduced here too.

Good fit if you:
Completed a Beginners session
Know the basics but want more
Are building toward Advanced
03
Advanced

The top tier. Students who can handle anything. Full dances, performance-ready skill, and the full depth of folk tradition.

Good fit if you:
Have mastered Intermediate material
Want performance-ready skills
Are ready for the hardest material
04
Adults

Open level for adults. High-energy, full-body, and built around real folk Bhangra.

Good fit if you:
Are curious about Bhangra
Want a real workout and learn choreographies to the most viral Punjabi songs
Have zero dance background, or are a professional dancer

Questions

Things people ask before joining.

Do I need to know Punjabi or Bhangra?+
No. Our Beginners and Adults tiers are built for people with zero background. You don't need to speak Punjabi, know any moves, or have any dance experience. Just show up.
What do I wear?+
Comfortable clothes you can move in. Athletic wear is fine. No special shoes or dance attire required for your first class. As you progress, we'll let you know if anything traditional is needed for performances.
My child is really young. Can they join?+
The minimum age for classes is 5. If your child is younger, fill out the form and select "my child is under 5" to share their birthdate. We'll reach out when they're ready.
How do I know which tier is right for us?+
When you submit the enrollment form, we'll ask a few questions and help place your student in the right tier. If you're not sure, just select "Not sure yet" and we'll figure it out together.
Can adults with no dance experience really keep up?+
Yes. The Adults class is designed for all levels, including complete beginners. Bhangra is high-energy but accessible. The first few sessions build the foundation. Everyone starts somewhere.
What happens after the free intro class?+
If you want to continue, you enroll for the full session. That covers the remaining 8 classes. No auto-renewals. Each session is a fresh decision.
Do you offer discounts for siblings or families?+
Yes, we offer family pricing for households enrolling 3 or more students. Contact us through the form or WhatsApp and we'll work out the details.
