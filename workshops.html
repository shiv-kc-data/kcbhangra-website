Workshops

Bhangra
for your crowd.

We come to you, or you join us. Two types of workshop, one goal: get people moving to folk Bhangra.

For organizations

We come to you.

Universities, corporate events, community organizations, schools, festivals. We build a workshop around your group, your space, and your audience.

No prior dance experience needed. No setup required. Works for groups of 10 to 200+.

Workshop format
What is folk Bhangra? Where it comes from, what it sounds like, why it's not Bollywood. No dancing yet. Just context.
Learn 2 to 3 choreographies Everyone participates. Real folk steps set to music your audience knows. No experience needed to keep up.
